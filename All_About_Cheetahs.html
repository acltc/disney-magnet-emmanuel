All About Cheetahs

One Fact

The cheetah is the fastest animal that lives on land.

The Speed of the Cheetah

A cheetah can go from 0 to 60 miles, but it has to take a break after running for one minute. One of the downfalls of the cheetahs.
The cheetah can make quick turns to eat its prey!!!

Hunting

Cheetahs are adopted to living in the grasslands and hot regions of Africa. Cheetahs can capture animals that other animals can't catch. Cheetahs temperature rise to 105 degrees during a chase.

Types of Cheetahs

The types of cheetahs are Acinonyx jubatus venaticus, Acinonyx jubatus hecki, Acinonyx jubatus soemmeringii, Acinonyx jubatus raineyii and Acinonyx jubatus raddei. The cat family it's in is Felidae.

Defense

Normally it will only fight other female cheetahs, but will run away from other predators. If it does fight and kill other predators it will immediately eat the predator. cheetahs have a 50% chance to kill their predators and prey. 90% of cheetah cubs get killed in their first few weeks.
